COVID-19 Policy

Since the beginning of the COVID19 pandemic, St. Lucyâ€™s Eye Care Center has consistently maintained safety measures to ensure a safe and healthy environment for our patients and staff.

Our Saftey Protocols Include

  • Temperature screening
  • Required mask wearing for all patients and companions who come in our office as well as our staff members
  • Complete disinfection of equipment in between patients
  • For social distancing, our appointments are spread out so there are less patients in the waiting room.
  • Frequent cleaning of office facilities
  • Hand sanitizer stations available throughout office
  • Patients will only be seen by Dr. Urmaza for treatment, diagnostic testing, procedures, and vision services. He is his own tech, with the exception of visual field tests. Seeing only one provider minimizes potential exposure.
