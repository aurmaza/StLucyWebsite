Meet Our Doctor

Benedict Urmaza MD, FAAO

Dr. Benedict Urmaza is a board-certified comprehensive ophthalmologist and ophthalmic surgeon with a background in internal medicine. With more than 20 years of experience, Dr. Urmaza's expertise includes treating chronic ocular diseases such as glaucoma, macular degeneration, cataracts and diabetic retinopathy.
